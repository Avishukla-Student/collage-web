Sports & Recreation

Our college is committed to the all-round development of students, offering excellent sports and recreation facilities. We encourage participation in a variety of indoor and outdoor games, fitness programs, and inter-college tournaments to promote teamwork, leadership, and a healthy lifestyle.

Outdoor Sports

  • Cricket (well-maintained pitch and practice nets)
  • Football (full-size ground)
  • Volleyball
  • Basketball
  • Badminton (outdoor courts)
  • Athletics (track and field events)

Indoor Sports & Fitness

  • Table Tennis
  • Chess & Carrom
  • Yoga and Aerobics Room
  • Modern Gymnasium with trainers

Events & Achievements

  • Annual Sports Meet with inter-departmental competitions
  • Participation in university and state-level tournaments
  • Special coaching camps and workshops
  • Recent Achievement: College Football Team - Runners-up in District Championship 2024
