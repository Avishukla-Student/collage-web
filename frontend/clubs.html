Clubs & Societies

Join various student-run clubs for arts, technology, culture, and more. Participate in events, competitions, and workshops to enhance your skills and network.

NCC (National Cadet Corps)

The NCC unit in our college aims to develop discipline, leadership, and a spirit of adventure among students. Cadets participate in drills, camps, social service, and national integration activities. NCC also provides opportunities for students to attend national camps and earn certificates that are valuable for future careers, especially in defense and civil services.

  • Regular parade and training sessions
  • Annual camps and adventure activities
  • Community service and awareness programs
  • Eligibility for NCC 'A', 'B', and 'C' certificates

NSS (National Service Scheme)

The NSS encourages students to engage in community service and social development. Volunteers participate in activities such as blood donation camps, cleanliness drives, literacy programs, and environmental awareness. NSS helps students develop a sense of social responsibility and leadership while making a positive impact on society.

  • Social service and outreach programs
  • Health and hygiene awareness campaigns
  • Tree plantation and environmental initiatives
  • Workshops and seminars on social issues
