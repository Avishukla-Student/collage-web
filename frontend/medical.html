Medical Services

Our college provides comprehensive medical services to ensure the health and well-being of all students and staff. The medical center is equipped to handle first aid, minor injuries, and routine health check-ups.

On-Campus Facilities:
  • Qualified medical officer and nursing staff available during college hours
  • First aid and emergency care for students and staff
  • Regular health check-up camps
  • Basic medicines and first aid supplies
  • Isolation room for students with contagious illnesses
Government Health Schemes (UP Govt):
  • Ayushman Bharat Yojana: Eligible students can avail free or subsidized treatment at empanelled hospitals under the Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY). For more details, visit pmjay.gov.in.
  • UP State Health Card: Students can apply for the Uttar Pradesh State Health Card to access government health facilities and schemes.
  • Free Vaccination Drives: The college, in collaboration with the UP Health Department, organizes free vaccination and health awareness camps.
  • Emergency Ambulance (UP Govt 108 Service): 24x7 ambulance service is available for emergencies. Dial 108 for immediate assistance.
