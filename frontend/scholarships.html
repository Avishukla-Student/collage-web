Scholarships & Financial Aid

Our college encourages students to apply for various scholarships and financial aid programs provided by the Government of Uttar Pradesh and the Government of India. These schemes are designed to support meritorious and economically weaker students.

UP Government Scholarships

  • Post Matric Scholarship (for SC/ST/OBC/Minority/General): Financial assistance for students studying in colleges and universities.
    Apply & Details: scholarship.up.gov.in
  • Pre Matric Scholarship: For students studying in classes 9 and 10.
    Apply & Details: scholarship.up.gov.in
  • Minority Welfare Scholarships: For students belonging to minority communities.
    Apply & Details: scholarship.up.gov.in
  • National Scholarship Portal (NSP): Central government scholarships for various categories.
    Apply & Details: scholarships.gov.in

Required Documents

  • Recent passport size photograph
  • Marksheet of last qualifying exam
  • Income certificate (as per scheme)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Bank passbook copy
  • Aadhaar card
