Faculty Services

Support and facilities available for faculty members in our college

Available Services

  • Research Support: Access to research grants, journals, and conference funding.
  • Faculty Development Programs: Workshops, seminars, and training for professional growth.
  • Library Access: Extensive resources including books, journals, and digital materials.
  • ICT Facilities: Computer labs, high-speed internet, and smart classrooms.
  • Administrative Support: Assistance with academic and administrative tasks.
  • Health & Wellness: On-campus medical facilities and wellness programs.
  • Collaboration Opportunities: Partnerships with industry and other academic institutions.
  • Leave & Sabbatical: Provision for various types of leave and sabbatical for research.
  • Transport Services: College transport for faculty commuting.
  • Recreational Facilities: Access to sports, gym, and cultural events.
  • Mentorship Programs: Opportunities to mentor students and junior faculty.
  • Consultancy Services: Support for faculty-led consultancy and industry projects.
  • Housing Assistance: Help with on-campus or nearby accommodation.
  • Financial Services: Salary management, loans, and retirement planning assistance.
  • Technical Support: IT helpdesk for hardware and software issues.
